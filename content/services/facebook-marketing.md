Facebook and Instagram ads can work extremely well or burn through a budget fast if the targeting, creative, and tracking aren't set up properly. We manage campaigns end-to-end, from pixel setup to creative testing to ongoing optimization.

We're not interested in vanity metrics like reach or impressions unless they're actually tied to a business outcome you care about leads, sales, or sign-ups.

## What's Included

- Meta Pixel / Conversions API setup so your tracking is actually accurate
- Audience research and targeting strategy
- Ad creative direction and copywriting (or we'll work with creative you already have)
- Campaign structure built for testing not one ad set and hoping for the best
- Ongoing optimization based on real performance data
- Clear, regular reporting tied to your actual business goals

## Who This Is For

Businesses that want paid social to be a predictable, measurable channel with someone actually watching the account, not a "set it and forget it" approach.

## How We Work

1. **Tracking First** — Meta Pixel and Conversions API set up and verified before a single rupee of ad spend. If you can't measure it, you can't optimize it.
2. **Audience & Offer Strategy** — Who we're targeting, what we're showing them, and what a profitable result looks like. We define target cost-per-result before launching.
3. **Creative & Campaign Build** — Ad copy and creative direction (or we work with your existing assets), structured into a testing framework — multiple angles, not one ad and hope.
4. **Launch & Optimize** — First 1-2 weeks are learning: cutting losers, scaling winners, refining audiences. Then ongoing weekly optimization.
5. **Report on What Matters** — Monthly reporting on cost per lead/sale and return on ad spend, in plain language. Not a screenshot of reach numbers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What budget do I need to start?**
Enough for the algorithm to learn — typically at least $10-20/day per campaign for lead generation, more for ecommerce purchases. Below that, results are too noisy to optimize. We'll tell you honestly if your budget is better spent elsewhere first.

**How long before ads become profitable?**
The first 2-4 weeks are testing — expect to pay for learning. Accounts that reach stable profitability usually do so within 1-2 months. If the offer or landing page is weak, no amount of ad optimization fixes it, and we'll flag that early.

**Do you create the ad images and videos?**
We handle static creative and direct video editing through our [design](/services/graphic-designing) and [video editing](/services/video-editing) services. Raw footage from your phone often outperforms polished studio content on Meta — we'll show you what to capture.

**iOS privacy changes — do Facebook ads still work?**
Yes, but tracking setup matters far more than it used to. That's why Conversions API (server-side tracking) is step one of our process, not an afterthought.
