Video gets attention that static images and text usually don't — but only if it's actually well edited. Shaky pacing, bad captions, or a video that doesn't hook viewers in the first three seconds gets scrolled past.

We edit for the platform it's going to, not just a generic cut. A 15-second Instagram Reel and a 90-second product ad need different pacing, different captions, and different hooks.

## What's Included

- Short-form editing for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts
- Ad video editing for Facebook/Instagram and Google campaigns
- Captions and subtitles (burned-in or platform-native)
- Color correction and basic audio cleanup
- Motion graphics and text overlays on brand
- Multiple format exports (vertical, square, widescreen) from one project

## Who This Is For

Businesses that already have raw footage — from a phone, a shoot, or UGC content — and need it turned into something polished enough to actually run as an ad or post.

## How We Work

1. **Footage Review & Brief** — Send us your raw footage and tell us where it's going (Reels, TikTok, YouTube, ad campaign). We'll tell you honestly whether the footage can achieve what you want — before you pay for editing.
2. **First Cut** — Edited for the platform: hook in the first 3 seconds for short-form, proper pacing for longer content, captions burned in where the platform muteness demands it.
3. **Revisions** — Timestamped feedback, defined revision rounds. You comment, we adjust.
4. **Multi-Format Delivery** — Vertical (9:16), square (1:1), and widescreen (16:9) exports from the same project where the content allows — one shoot, every placement covered.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What footage do I need to provide?**
Phone footage is genuinely fine for social content — often better than studio footage for ads, because it reads as authentic. We'll send you a simple shot list of what to capture (angles, lighting basics, lengths) so the raw material cuts together well.

**How fast is turnaround?**
Single short-form videos: usually 2-4 working days. Batches (e.g., a month of Reels) are scheduled on a rolling calendar so you always have content ready to post.

**Do you do captions and subtitles?**
Always for short-form — most social video is watched muted. Styled, accurately-timed captions matched to your brand fonts and colors, burned-in or as platform-native files, whichever fits your workflow.

**Can you edit a batch from one long recording?**
Yes — one of the most cost-effective things we do. A single 30-minute talking-head recording or event shoot typically yields 8-15 clips worth posting.
