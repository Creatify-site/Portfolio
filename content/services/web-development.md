Your website is usually the first real interaction a potential customer has with your business. If it's slow, dated, or hard to navigate on a phone, you're losing customers before they even see what you offer.

We build custom websites focused on three things: speed, clarity, and conversion. That means clean code instead of bloated page builders, a structure that makes sense to actual visitors, and calls-to-action that are easy to find instead of buried in a menu.

## What's Included

- Custom design and development — no generic templates
- Mobile-first, responsive layouts that work on every screen size
- Fast load times (we test against Core Web Vitals, not just "it looks fine")
- Clean, maintainable code your next developer won't have to untangle
- Basic on-page SEO setup from day one — proper headings, meta tags, and image optimization
- Post-launch support for bug fixes and small updates

## Who This Is For

Businesses that need a site that actually works for them not just something to point to. Whether it's a marketing site, a landing page for a campaign, or a full multi-page business site, we scope it to what you actually need.

## How We Work

1. **Discovery** — A short call or email thread to understand what the site needs to do: who visits it, what action they should take, and what's not working today. We'll also review your current site (if you have one) for what's worth keeping.
2. **Scope & Quote** — You get a written scope with a fixed price and timeline. No hourly billing surprises, no vague "phase 2" placeholders.
3. **Design & Build** — We design and build in short cycles, sharing a live preview link early so you're seeing real progress on a real URL — not waiting weeks for a big reveal.
4. **Review & Refine** — You click through everything on the preview, we fix and polish. Content, spacing, mobile behavior, load speed.
5. **Launch & Handover** — We deploy, connect your domain, verify analytics, and hand over everything: code, access, and a short guide for making basic updates yourself.

Most marketing sites take 2-4 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on how many pages and how quickly content decisions get made.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What platform do you build on?**
Whatever actually fits the job: static site generators (like Hugo or Astro) for fast marketing sites, WordPress when your team needs a familiar editor, or Shopify when you're selling products. We don't force one stack on every project.

**Do I own the website when it's done?**
Yes — completely. Code, domain, hosting account, analytics. There's no lock-in and no proprietary builder you can't leave.

**Can you work with my existing branding?**
Yes. If you have a logo, colors, and fonts, we build around them. If you don't, our [graphic design service](/services/graphic-designing) can create the basics first.

**What about hosting and maintenance?**
We'll set you up on modern hosting (often free or nearly free for marketing sites) and offer an optional maintenance arrangement for updates and small changes. Many clients need only a few hours a month.
