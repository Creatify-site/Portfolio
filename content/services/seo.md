SEO isn't a switch you flip once — it's the compounding result of a site that's technically sound, content that actually answers what people are searching for, and a few fundamentals done consistently over time.

We start with the technical side (crawlability, site speed, structured data, mobile experience) because content strategy built on a broken technical foundation won't rank no matter how good it is. From there, we work on the on-page and content work that actually moves rankings.

## What's Included

- Technical SEO audit — crawlability, indexability, site speed, Core Web Vitals
- Keyword research grounded in real search intent, not just volume
- On-page optimization: titles, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal linking
- Structured data (schema markup) so search engines understand your content
- Content strategy and blog support to build topical authority
- Monthly reporting so you can see what's actually working

## Who This Is For

Businesses that want organic traffic to be a real, growing channel — not businesses looking for a guaranteed #1 ranking overnight (nobody can honestly promise that, and you should be skeptical of anyone who does).
