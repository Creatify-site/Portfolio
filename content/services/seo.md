**SEO isn't a switch you flip once** it's the compounding result of a site that's technically sound, content that actually answers what people are searching for, and a few fundamentals done consistently over time.

We start with the technical side (crawlability, site speed, structured data, mobile experience) because content strategy built on a broken technical foundation won't rank no matter how good it is. From there, we work on the on-page and content work that actually moves rankings.

## What's Included

- Technical SEO audit — crawlability, indexability, site speed, Core Web Vitals
- Keyword research grounded in real search intent, not just volume
- On-page optimization: titles, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal linking
- Structured data (schema markup) so search engines understand your content
- Content strategy and blog support to build topical authority
- Monthly reporting so you can see what's actually working

## Who This Is For

Businesses that want organic traffic to be a real, growing channel not businesses looking for a guaranteed #1 ranking overnight (nobody can honestly promise that, and you should be skeptical of anyone who does).

## How We Work

1. **Technical Audit** — Crawlability, indexability, site speed, structured data, mobile experience. You get a prioritized findings list, not a 40-page PDF of generic advice.
2. **Fix the Foundation** — We fix the technical issues first (or hand your developer a precise punch list). Content built on a broken foundation doesn't rank.
3. **Keyword & Content Plan** — Keyword research mapped to actual pages: what each page should target, what's missing, and what to write next — prioritized by realistic difficulty, not vanity volume.
4. **On-Page Optimization** — Titles, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal linking, and schema markup across your key pages.
5. **Measure & Iterate Monthly** — Rankings, organic traffic, and — more importantly — leads and sales from organic. We adjust the plan based on what the data shows.

Expect first movement in 6-12 weeks for low-competition terms; competitive terms take months. Anyone promising faster is guessing or lying.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long until I see results?**
Technical fixes can show impact within weeks (especially if something was blocking indexing). Content-driven growth compounds over 3-6 months. We set expectations per keyword upfront — some are quick wins, some are long games.

**Do you guarantee first-page rankings?**
No, and you should be suspicious of anyone who does. Google's results change constantly and nobody outside Google controls them. What we do guarantee: transparent monthly reporting on the exact metrics that matter, and honest recalibration when something isn't working.

**Is SEO still worth it with AI search (ChatGPT, AI Overviews)?**
Yes — arguably more. AI answers are built on crawled, well-structured web content, and the sites that get cited are the ones with clean technical SEO and genuinely useful pages. The fundamentals transfer directly.

**Do you write the content too?**
We can — blog posts, service pages, landing pages — or we can brief your writer with exact specifications (target keyword, structure, questions to answer). Both models work; it depends on who knows your business best.
