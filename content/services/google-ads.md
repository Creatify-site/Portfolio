Businesses ready to invest in paid search with someone actively managing the account — reviewing search terms, adjusting bids, and cutting what isn't working, not just launching a campaign and checking back monthly.

## How We Work

1. **Conversion Tracking Setup** — Google Ads conversions, GA4 events, and Enhanced Conversions verified before launch. Most underperforming accounts we inherit were optimizing toward the wrong (or no) conversion signal.
2. **Keyword & Competitor Research** — What people actually search, what it costs, and what competitors bid on — mapped against your margins so we target terms that can actually be profitable.
3. **Campaign Build** — Tightly-themed ad groups, ad copy matched to search intent, and landing page recommendations. For ecommerce: Merchant Center feed setup and Shopping campaigns.
4. **Negative Keywords & Waste Control** — Ongoing search-term review to cut spend on irrelevant queries — usually the fastest ROI improvement in any account.
5. **Optimize & Report** — Weekly bid/budget adjustments, monthly reporting on cost per acquisition and return on ad spend.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Google Ads or Facebook Ads — which should I run?**
Google captures existing demand (people searching for what you sell); Facebook creates demand (interrupting people who weren't looking). If people already search for your product/service, start with Google. If it's novel or impulse-driven, start with [Facebook](/services/facebook-marketing). Many businesses eventually run both.

**I ran Google Ads before and lost money. Why would this be different?**
The usual culprits: broad match keywords with no negatives, no real conversion tracking, and ads pointing at a generic homepage. All three are fixable — and we audit any existing account free before quoting, so you'll know exactly what went wrong.

**What's a realistic budget?**
Depends entirely on your cost-per-click and margins — a Multan services business and an international ecommerce store are different worlds. We'll estimate your CPCs during research and tell you the minimum viable budget before you commit.

**Do you handle Performance Max campaigns?**
Yes, where they fit — usually for ecommerce alongside standard Shopping. PMax without proper conversion tracking and feed optimization burns budget fast, so we make sure the foundation is in place first.
