Google Ads can put you in front of people actively searching for what you sell but only if the campaign structure, keyword targeting, and conversion tracking are set up correctly. A lot of accounts we've reviewed are quietly burning budget on broad match keywords with no real tracking in place.

We build campaigns around actual business outcomes: sales, leads, or bookings not just click-through rate.

## What's Included

- Conversion tracking setup (Google Ads, Google Analytics, and Enhanced Conversions)
- Search campaign strategy and keyword research
- Shopping campaign setup for ecommerce (Merchant Center feed included)
- Ad copywriting and landing page recommendations
- Negative keyword management to stop wasted spend
- Regular reporting tied to cost-per-acquisition and return on ad spend

## Who This Is For

Businesses ready to invest in paid search with someone actively managing the account — reviewing search terms, adjusting bids, and cutting what isn't working, not just launching a campaign and checking back monthly.
