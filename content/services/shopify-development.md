Shopify makes it easy to launch a store — it doesn't make it easy to launch a store that actually converts, loads fast, and doesn't fight you every time you want to change something. That's where we come in.

We work directly in Liquid, not just app settings, which means we can build the custom sections, filters, and functionality that off-the-shelf themes and apps can't give you — without stacking five paid apps on top of your theme.

## What's Included

- Custom theme development and customization (Dawn and other Online Store 2.0 themes)
- Custom sections, filters, and product page features built in Liquid
- App integration and cleanup — we'll tell you honestly if you don't need an app for something
- Store migrations and Shopify Plus support
- Performance optimization for faster collection and product pages
- Ongoing maintenance and support after launch

## Who This Is For

Ecommerce brands that have outgrown a stock theme, or are launching a new store and want it built right the first time — not patched together with apps that slow down your site.
