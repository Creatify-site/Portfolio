## Which Platform Fits Your Business?

**Google** captures existing demand: people actively searching for your product or service. If people already search for what you sell, this is usually the first place to invest.

**Facebook and Instagram** create demand: putting your offer in front of people who were not looking but fit your customer profile. Strong for ecommerce, local services, and lead generation.

**TikTok** rewards attention and authenticity. Raw, native style video often outperforms polished ads here, and costs per view are still lower than the older platforms. Best for products with visual appeal and younger audiences.

Most businesses do not need all three on day one. We recommend a starting platform based on where your customers actually are, then expand once the first channel is profitable.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What budget do I need to start?**
Enough for the algorithm to learn, typically at least 10 to 20 dollars per day per campaign. Below that, results are too noisy to optimize. We will tell you honestly if your budget is better spent elsewhere first.

**How long before ads become profitable?**
The first 2 to 4 weeks are testing, so expect to pay for learning. Accounts that reach stable profitability usually do so within 1 to 2 months. If the offer or landing page is weak, no amount of ad optimization fixes it, and we flag that early.

**I ran ads before and lost money. Why would this be different?**
The usual culprits: no real conversion tracking, broad targeting with no testing structure, and ads pointing at a generic homepage. All three are fixable. We audit any existing ad account free before quoting, so you know exactly what went wrong.

**Do you create the ad images and videos?**
Yes, through our [design](/services/graphic-designing) and [video editing](/services/video-editing) services. For TikTok and Reels, footage from your phone often outperforms studio content, and we will show you exactly what to capture.
