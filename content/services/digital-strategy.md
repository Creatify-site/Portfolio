A new website, an SEO push, and a round of Facebook ads can all be individually "fine" and still not add up to much if they're not pointed at the same goal. Digital strategy is the layer that connects them deciding what to prioritize, in what order, and how to measure whether it's actually working.

We help businesses figure out where to focus first: is the website the bottleneck, is it traffic, is it conversion once people land on the site? Then we build a plan around the answer instead of doing a little of everything at once.

## What's Included

- Current-state audit across your website, SEO, and any active ad campaigns
- Prioritization: what to fix first, and why
- A clear roadmap with realistic timelines, not vague promises
- KPI definition so you know what "working" actually looks like
- Coordination across the specific services you need (web, SEO, ads, design)
- Regular check-ins to adjust the plan as real data comes in

## Who This Is For

Businesses that want a plan before they spend on execution; especially if you've tried a few one-off marketing efforts before and aren't sure what actually moved the needle.

## How We Work

1. **Current-State Audit** — Website, analytics, SEO, and any active ad accounts reviewed together. Where is traffic coming from, where does it leak, and what's actually driving revenue today?
2. **Find the Bottleneck** — Every business has one biggest constraint: not enough traffic, traffic that doesn't convert, or conversions that don't repeat. Strategy means fixing that one first — not doing a little of everything.
3. **Roadmap** — A prioritized plan with realistic timelines: what to do this month, this quarter, and what to deliberately ignore for now. Each item tied to the specific service or fix it needs.
4. **KPI Definition** — Agree on the numbers that define "working" before any execution starts — cost per lead, conversion rate, organic growth rate — so progress is measurable, not vibes.
5. **Execute & Review** — We execute through our own services or coordinate with your team, then review against the KPIs monthly and adjust.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is this different from just buying your other services?**
Order and focus. A business that needs conversion fixes doesn't benefit from more ad spend; one with a broken site shouldn't start with SEO content. Strategy decides the sequence — which often *saves* money by cutting work you don't need yet.

**Is this a one-time plan or ongoing?**
Either. Some clients want a one-time roadmap they execute internally; others keep us on monthly to run the plan and adjust it as data comes in. The roadmap is yours regardless.

**What if I already have a marketing team?**
Then we work as the outside eyes: audit, prioritize, define the KPIs, and hand your team a plan they execute. No territorial games — we'll happily make your in-house team look good.

**What does the audit cover exactly?**
Analytics configuration (is your data even trustworthy?), website conversion path, technical SEO health, content gaps, ad account efficiency if you're running paid — and the competitive picture for your niche.
