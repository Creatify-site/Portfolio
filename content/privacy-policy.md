This policy explains what information Creatify collects when you visit this website or work with us, and how that information is used.

## Information We Collect

**Contact form.** When you submit our contact form, we receive the name, email address, and project details you enter.

**Booking a call.** When you book a free audit through our scheduling tool (Cal.com), we receive the name, email address, timezone, and any notes you provide for that meeting.

**Website analytics.** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. This collects general usage data such as pages viewed, time on site, device type, and approximate location, using cookies. This data is anonymized and aggregated, we don't use it to identify individual visitors personally.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to respond to inquiries, schedule and conduct consultation calls, deliver the services you've hired us for, and understand how people use our website so we can improve it. We do not sell your information to third parties.

## Third Party Services

We rely on a small number of trusted third party tools to run this site and our business:

- **Google Analytics**, for website usage statistics
- **Cal.com**, for scheduling consultation calls
- **Email**, for direct communication about your project

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data on their platforms.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics to understand site traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not work as expected if you do.

## Data Retention

We keep contact form submissions and booking details for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or deliver the service you've engaged us for, and for a reasonable period afterward for record keeping.

## Your Rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, at any time by emailing us at [Support.creatify@gmail.com](mailto:Support.creatify@gmail.com).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally as our services or tools change. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

## Contact
